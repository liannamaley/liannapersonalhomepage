About Me

Lianna Maley is an artist based out of New Paltz, NY. She is currently in her third year at SUNY studying graphic design with a minor in psychology. Taking on a major switch in her college career from psychobiology to the world of art, college has most certainly been a journey for her. Although the sudden major switch to Graphic esign seems sudden, art is far from new for Lianna. Coming from a long line of artists it is no wonder that she made the obvious choice to switch over. Lianna is a muralist and graphic designer and has worked with many local companies to create unique pieces to represent brands as well as to light up a space with art.
